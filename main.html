"Why I want to become a devloper?"

Passion for Technology: You may enjoy working with technology and have a natural curiosity about how things work. Software development allows you to build tools and applications that make a real impact. Problem-Solving: If you love solving complex problems, software development provides endless challenges. Every project is unique, requiring analytical and creative thinking to find the best solutions. Creative Freedom: Software development is not just technical; it's creative. You can build anything from a simple app to a large-scale platform, turning your ideas into reality. Continuous Learning: The tech industry evolves rapidly, and as a developer, you get the opportunity to keep learning new technologies, frameworks, and methodologies, which keeps the work exciting. Impactful Career: As a developer, you can create tools that help people, automate tedious tasks, or bring convenience to daily life. The sense of contributing to something larger is highly fulfilling.
